# Child Safety Standards Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-02

> This page describes haru's standards for addressing Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

## 1. Core Principle

haru maintains a zero-tolerance approach to Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM). Sexual content or conduct involving minors is prohibited, and accounts found in violation may have their access restricted.

## 2. Age Requirement

haru is intended for users aged 18 or older (or the age of majority in the user's country of residence) and does not allow use by minors.

## 3. Reporting

Inappropriate behavior by other users can be reported through the in-app report and block features or via the contact below. Reports received are reviewed and acted upon as needed.

## 4. Legal Compliance

haru complies with applicable child safety laws and cooperates with lawful requests from the relevant authorities.
